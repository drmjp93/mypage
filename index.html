About the Physician

A physician who listens,
then acts.

Dr. Mit Panchani is a qualified Internal Medicine physician with an MBBS foundation and a postgraduate MD in General Medicine. His additional credential in Industrial Health (PGCIH) and fellowship in 2D Echocardiography give him a uniquely broad clinical lens — from cardiac imaging to occupational and chronic-disease management.

He has attended over 9175309 cases, performed more than 1,711 echocardiographic studies, and provided pre-operative surgical fitness assessments for 1,676 patients. His practice covers diabetes, thyroid disorders, hypertension, cholesterol, cardiac conditions, rheumatoid arthritis, vertigo, respiratory illnesses, renal infections, and headache disorders — with particular attention to complex chronic-disease patients including those in pregnancy.

He is skilled in ECG interpretation and 2D Echocardiography, and provides preventive health counselling alongside long-term chronic disease follow-up. He believes in clear communication, honest prognosis, and cost-aware prescribing — ensuring patients understand not just the diagnosis but the path forward.
